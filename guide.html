How to use

8-queens simulator

To begin, click the Solve button.

The algorithm will try to place one queen in each row of the chessboard such that no two queens threaten each other. We can see a live log of each step taken.

Once all queens are successfully placed, the board will display a celebration animation.

To try again, just click the Reset button.
